About Me

I'm Raine Eera, a 20 year old multi-talent artist with a focus & obsession in music. Immense passion and interest in creativity, art expression, sound design, getting what I want out of existence, and sharing my weird lens of the world with others alike and unalike. Whether that'd be with a song, art piece, photograph, or multiform combinations...To quote Tom Hanks, "Ya never know whatcha gonna get".

Condensing myself into an about page is a difficult task, but to put it simply: I am a swiss army knife of creativity. I love creating. I love expressing myself. I love the thought of making people both understood, comforted, and uncomfortable through what I do. It's part of why I get up out of bed in the morning. Staying down to earth, open hearted, and open minded is what is vital to me. Teaching creativity and spreading inspiration through art is my motivation for even starting this website. If you vibe with that, come see what I do and reach out.

On the site you'll find my blog that I update frequently on what I have to say or have learned, showcases of each art form I pursue, links to where you can find me, and where to enjoy everything I release!

If you love my work and want to buy my digital products, they are all available in my 3 or more dollar monthly subscription. Including all the instruments and patches I make, all of my music in high quality download (+ stems, just contact me) my curated playlists full of different flavors and genres, and of course your name in all my projects as a personal thank you!
